What's Gabber?

Gabber is an exploratory application that captures and shares conversations within communities. Data produced is fed back into the community for analysis and use. Gabber is currently deployed across various domain-specific contexts, ranging from education, where Gabber is used to capture, understand and respond to students learning needs and issues as they arise, and embedded within consultation processes as a resource for representing citizens experiences of using the Metro in Tyne and Wear.

Sharing your experiences, collecting the experiences of others, and having them all in one place enables you as a community to engage with and understand the needs and concerns of others within this community. By representing all of these perspectives in one place, they could be used to engage local government in decision-making processes, such as where to place new buildings, or understand local citizens concerns.
