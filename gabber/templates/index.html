About Gabber

Gabber was created as an exploratory application to capture and share conversations between communities. Data produced through gabber is fed back into the community for analysis and use.

Gabber is currently being used to collect and share experiences of volunteering with an incentive model embedded within it. If you are interested in capturing your friends experiences and earning money for your time, then Gabber is for you.
